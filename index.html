20-Week Hyper-Anabolic Protocol

Mission Objectives: Recomposition & Regrowth

This protocol is engineered to aggressively leverage myonuclei retention (muscle memory) over 20 weeks, converting stored energy into high-quality muscle fiber.

Performance Goals

  • Primary Objective: Achieve significant body recomposition (aggressive fat loss with maximal muscle gain).
  • Weakness Eradication: Systematically eliminate the arm weakness via high-frequency training.
  • Initial Target: Transition from 205 lb to a lean, conditioned 215–225 lb by Week 20.

Protocol Phase Architecture

  • Phase 1 (Weeks 1–4): High volume, lower intensity, tissue stabilization.
  • Phase 2 (Weeks 5–12): Increased load, PPL ×2, Nandrolone initiated for joint support.
  • Phase 3 (Weeks 13–20): Body-part split, RPE 9–10, maximal caloric/compound saturation.

Training Protocol Overload

Primary Focus: Progressive Overload & High-Frequency Arms

Phase 1: Re-Awakening (Weeks 1–4)

RPE 7 (3 reps in reserve), 15–20 Reps. Focus on mind-muscle connection and controlled negatives (3-second eccentric).

Day Focus Exercise Sets x Reps Notes
Day 1 Upper (Pull Focus) Machine Pullover, Seated Cable Row, Cable Face Pull, Hammer Curl (Machines), Cable Tricep Pushdown 3x15–20, 3x15–20, 3x20, 3x15–20, 3x15–20 Controlled 3s negative on rows. High-rep rotator cuff activation.
Day 2 Lower (Quad Focus) Leg Extension (Pre-Exhaust), Leg Press, Standing Calf Raise, Machine Hip Adduction 3x15–20, 3x15–20, 4x20, 3x20 Feet low on leg press, deep stretch.
Day 3 Upper (Push Focus) Incline Dumbbell Press, Pec Deck Fly, Dumbbell Lateral Raise, Machine Overhead Press 3x15–20, 3x15–20, 3x20, 3x15–20 Light, strict control. Maximize blood flow with flyes and raises.
Day 4 OFF REST — Mandatory Mobility/Foam Rolling.
Day 5 Full Body Primer Goblet Squat (Deep Stretch), Dumbbell Bench Press, Cable Row (Wide Grip), EZ Bar Curl / Pushdown (Superset) 3x15, 3x15, 3x15, 2x20 (each) Focus on deep stretch in the squat. Arm finisher.
Day 6 Lower (Ham Focus) Seated Leg Curl, 45-Degree Hyperextension, Seated Calf Raise, Hanging Leg Raise 3x15–20, 3x15–20, 4x20, 3x15 Focus on glute/ham contraction on hyperextensions.
Day 7 Upper (Volume Pump) Cable Crossover (High to Low), Dumbbell Pullover, Seated Dumbbell Curl, Rope Tricep Extension 3x15, 3x15, 3x15, 3x15 High frequency arm work.

Phase 2: Intensity Drive (Weeks 5–12)

RPE 8–9 (1–2 reps in reserve), 8–12 Reps. Introduce Nandrolone, BFR, and higher loads.

Day Focus Exercise Sets x Reps Techniques

Day 2 PULL (Back/Biceps) Weighted Pull-up/Lat Pulldown, T-Bar Row (Heavy), Cable Seated Row, Dumbbell Shrug, Barbell Curl, Cable Curl 4x8–10, 4x8–10, 3x10–12, 3x12, 3x8–10, 1x100 Reps Focus on stretch/squeeze on rows. Rest-Pause Finisher on Cable Curl.

Phase 3: Peak Overload (Weeks 13–20)

RPE 9–10 (Failure sets), 6–12 Reps. Body-Part Split. Maximize advanced techniques.

Day Focus Exercise Sets x Reps Techniques
Day 1 CHEST & CALVES Barbell Flat Bench Press, Incline Dumbbell Press, Cable Crossover, Machine Calf Press 4x6–8, 3x8–10, 3x12–15, 5x10–15 Forced Reps on Bench. Controlled Negatives on DB Press. Intra-set stretching on Crossovers.
Day 2 BACK Weighted Pull-ups, Barbell Bent-Over Row (Heavy), Lat Pulldown (V-Bar), Machine Pullover 4x8–10, 4x6–8, 3x10–12, 3x12–15 Failure set on Pull-ups. Heavy, strict form on Barbell Row.
Day 3 LEGS (Full) Barbell Squat (High Bar), Leg Press, Seated Leg Curl, Leg Extension 4x6–8, 3x10 + Drop x 2, 3x10–12, 3x15 + Rest Pause Heavy load on Squat. Triple Drop Set on Leg Press. Rest-Pause Finisher on Extensions.

Day 5 SHOULDERS & ARMS Seated Dumbbell Press, Dumbbell Lateral Raise, Bent Over Rear Delt Fly, Arm Giant Set 4x8–10, 4x10–15, 3x15, 3 rounds of 10 reps Forced Reps on DB Press. Giant Set: Barbell Curl, Tricep Extension, Hammer Curl, Rope Pushdown (No rest).
Day 6 OFF REST — Full recovery.
Day 7 OFF REST — Full recovery.
